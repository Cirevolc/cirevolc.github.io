Boston Crime Stats 2015-2018

This is some d3.js graphics demonstrating crime rate differences among Boston neighborhoods. This visualization reveals overall crime rate, including both violent and non-violent crime, have not been improved among most of the major neighborhoods in Boston. Some areas have pretty high violent crime ratio. Maybe the government and the police need to work harder on this.
